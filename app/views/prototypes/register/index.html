Register a new delegate

Use this process to register either a single person, or a group of people, onto the platform. You will be able to:

  • Enrol them as a learner onto a specific self assessment.
  • Promote them to an admin role.
  • Schedule a welcome email to invite them to access the platform.

Before you start

You will need the following information for each person you're registering:

  • Full name
  • Email address

It's also recommended that you have their professional registration number, but if you don't have this now then it can be added at a later time.
