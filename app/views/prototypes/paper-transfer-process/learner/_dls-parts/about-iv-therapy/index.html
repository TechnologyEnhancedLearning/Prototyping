IV Therapy Passport

Practice Learning and Assessment

This platform hosts Step 3 of the IV Therapy Passport pathway, which focuses on practice learning and assessment.

Upon completion, you will receive a digital IV Therapy Passport that is transferable between organisations.

Prerequisites before starting Step 3: Supervised Clinical Practice:

Before beginning Step 3 of the IV Therapy Passport pathway, you must complete the following prerequisites:

  • met organisational requisites to administer non-intravenous medication
  • complete equipment training and assessment
  • complete Infection Prevention and Control and Aseptic Technique Training
  • read and understand Organisational policy and guidance on administration of intravenous medication
  • Step 1: Completed all 7 IV Therapy Passport eLearning sessions on the elearning for Healthcare Hub (elfh)
  • Step 2: Passed all 7 corresponding eAssessments on the elfh hub

Selecting Proficiencies:

  1. Core and Optional Proficiencies:
    • you must achieve all core proficiencies
    • additionally, select at least 1 proficiency in each of the following categories after discussing with your Manager/Educator:
      • method of preparation
      • method of administration
      • vascular access device proficiency
    • select all proficiencies relevant or required for your role
    • optional proficiencies can be added over time if your scope of practice changes
  2. Recording Completion:
    • use the designated area in the proficiency list to record:
      • completion of prerequisite learning
      • completion of elearning and eAssessments

Assessment Process:

  1. Recording Assessments:
    • there are 4 opportunities to record assessments
    • you must achieve proficiency in at least the first and final assessments
    • optional assessments are available for practice and your organisation may require their completion
  2. Assessment Steps:
    • select an assessment: first, optional, or final
    • perform a self-assessment of your performance against the proficiency, indicating whether you are practising or have achieved it
    • submit the proficiencies for which you require confirmation of achievement from your assessor.
      • multiple proficiencies can be submitted simultaneously
    • your assessor will log into the supervision platform to confirm your proficiency
  3. If Proficiency is not confirmed:
    • the assessor will record 'rejected' and provide an action plan

Declaration:

  • once you have achieved both the first and final assessments for each selected proficiency, complete a 'self-declaration' of proficiency and use the manage confirmation request function to send this to an educator or manager
  • this declaration must be signed off by your educator or manager to finalise your IV Therapy Passport

Transfer:

  • present your completed proficiencies when transferring between roles or organisations
  • you may select additional optional proficiencies to complete as required by your new role
